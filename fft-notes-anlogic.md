### **FFT**

$$
X(k)=\sum_{n=0}^{N-1}x(n)W_{N}^{nk} ~~~~~~~~~~0\le k \le N-1\\
x(n)=\frac1N \sum_{k=0}^{N-1}X(k)W_{N}^{-nk}  ~~~~~~~~~~0\le n \le N-1
$$

补码相乘的符号位给怎么表示？

**利用补码做乘法时，需要把数据进行符号位扩展，让操作数的位宽等于目标结果的宽度。**

When multiplying two's complement numbers, you have to perform sign extensions to the operands to meet the number of digits your multiplication will yield, i.e., in your case  4+4=8  digits.

11111110×00000111=111100102111111102×000001112=111100102

As there are 2∗32∗3 fractional bits, the result is 1.1100102=−1426=−0.218751.1100102=−1426=−0.21875. Normalizing this number to 33 fractional bits in Q0.3 format yields 1.1102=−0.251.1102=−0.25.



需要关注的问题：符号位的扩展、舍入、归一化、小数位（整数位）的扩展，精度、溢出等
